Get Started

Sebastian Montgomery-Smythe

Our CEO founded Hot Beans with a singular, unwavering vision: to dismantle the financial and geographical barriers surrounding high-quality tech education and decisively bridge the ever-growing global skills gap in the digital economy. Having personally navigated the complexities of launching a successful tech career, he witnessed firsthand the immense transformative power of coding—not just as a professional skill, but as a tool for economic mobility and creativity. However, he was equally aware of the pervasive lack of accessible, industry-relevant training available to talented individuals outside of traditional, expensive university pathways or major urban centers. Hot Beans was thus conceived not merely as a training platform, but as a commitment to fostering a truly diverse and inclusive tech community. His motivation was rooted in the deep conviction that talent is universally distributed, though opportunity often is not. By focusing on practical, project-based learning and direct industry mentorship, Hot Beans aims to empower individuals from all backgrounds—whether they are career switchers, recent graduates, or self-taught enthusiasts—to master cutting-edge technologies, seize control of their futures, and become the innovative leaders who will shape the next era of digital development.
